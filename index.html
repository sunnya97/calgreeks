UC Berkeley CalGreeks

CalGreeks is the overarching organization for the UC Berkeley Greek Community and is built on the four pillars of Friendship, Leadership, Scholarship and Service. Our community is comprised of 3,400 life-long, undergraduate members in over 60 local, national, and international fraternities and sororities.

InterFraternity Council

The IFC is the binding organization that ties together the 33 recognized fraternity chapters on campus. It provides its 1300 undergradute men with opportunities within an intimate community in order to encourage success on campus and in today’s global society. IFC serves as the official connection between the individual chapters and the university and is self-governed through the efforts of representative delegates from each chapter.

Learn more

PanHellenic Council

The Panhellenic Council is the governing body for 15 sorority chapters on campus comprised of a board of student officers elected each year. Thirteen chapters are associated with the National Panhellenic Conference (an umbrella organization for 26 international women's fraternities and sororities), one chapter is associated with the National Association of Latino Fraternal Organizations, and one is a multicultural asian-interest associate chapter.

Learn more

Multicultural Greek Council

The Multi-Cultural Greek Council at the UC Berkeley was founded on February 2007 in order to better meet our individual needs, voice our concerns and promote the greater good of our community. As culturally based organizations, we strive to promote and uplift our common heritages and history. As a council we shall uphold our commitment to service, scholarship and mutual respect and cultural awareness.

Learn more

National PanHellenic Council

The National Pan-Hellenic Council is the umbrella organization for the 9 historically African American fraternities and sororities. The stated purpose of the organization as written in 1930 is, “Unanimity of thought and action, as far as possible, in the conduct of Greek letter collegiate fraternities and sororities, and to consider problems of mutual interest to its member organizations.” NPHC members work together to promote unity, share ideas, discuss common goals, and engage in public service.

Order of Omega

Order of Omega is a leadership honor society for members of Fraternity and Sorority organizations. Members are selected from the top 3% of the CalGreeks community in areas of scholarship, leadership, and involvement within their respective organizations.

Learn more

Greening the Greeks

Greening the Greeks is a student organization consisting of Cal Greek members that promote environmental awareness and sustainability within the fraternity and sorority community, especially in housing facilities and at Greek-sponsored events.

Learn more

GAMMA

Greeks Advocating the Mature Management of Alcohol (GAMMA) is an organization dedicated to fostering an atmosphere built around safe socializing that reflects the values of the Greek Community. GAMMA provides resources and support to those chapters that choose to have alcohol at events and regulates these events. Each fraternity and sorority is required to have a representative in the organization.

Learn more

Greek Opportunity Fund

The Greek Opportunity Fund, operated by the ASUC provides monetary support to empower students and chapters of CalGreeks to engage in meaningful community-based initiatives which focus on community service and philanthropy, scholarship and educational enhancement, leadership development, professional and career development, and/or health and safety, including alcohol education.
